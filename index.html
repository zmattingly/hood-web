The USS Hood

We're a Discord server run by Friends of DeSoto for Friends of DeSoto.

The Hood provides a lively place to chat with fellow fans of the fine line of Uxbridge-Shimoda products and discuss Pod, Trek, and Miriam other topics.

We have an active, friendly crew and strive to create a welcoming and safe environment for All. FoDs. Everywhere.

The Drive

Every year for the MaxFunDrive we collect donations from members, and use the funds to give MaxFun gift memberships to folks who might not otherwise be able to support Greatest Gen and Greatest Trek.

The number of gift memberships available depends entirely on how many donations we receive.

Any excess funds which cannot be distributed at the end of the drive this year will be donated to The American Brain Tumor Association, in remembrance of our friend Dr. Z

How To Participate

This year we'd like to invite you to join us!

Anyone can donate by visiting the Cheddar Up.

If you'd like a chance to win a membership all you have to do is join the Discord server and then complete a short form! Further details and a link to the form can be found in the #gift-membership-drive channel on the server.

More Info

In order to register gift memberships for the lucky winners, Maximum Fun does require the recipient's email address. Your address will be requested in the signup form but we will not use this information for any purpose other than registration and, if needed, to contact you to alert you that you've won. All email addresses and other information will be deleted once the drive is over.

If you have any other questions, please don't hesitate to hop in the Discord and flag down a mod by tagging @Commander and/or @Captain. We're here to help!
